Calculator




This calculator will give you an overview of how your body weight compares to general values. The Body Mass Index (BMI) is calculated as your weight (in kilograms) divided by the square of your height (in meters).

This calculator is intended for use by adults only (pregnant or breastfeeding women should not rely on this BMI reading), and no actions should be taken based on the result without first consulting a qualified professional, such as a doctor.
